# Student Microelectronics Foundry

The **Student Microelectronics Foundry (SMF)** is a student-organized platform for practical microelectronics, semiconductor fabrication and microsystems engineering.

The documentation collects:

- organizational information;
- safety documentation;
- fabrication processes;
- equipment instructions;
- experiments and student projects.

## Main areas

- Semiconductor device fabrication
- Photolithography and microstructuring
- Doping, oxidation, etching and metallization
- Electrical characterization
- Open-source laboratory equipment
- Teaching and student projects

!!! warning "Documentation is not authorization"

    Laboratory work may only be performed after the required safety training, equipment instruction and approval.
